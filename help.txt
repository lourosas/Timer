LTimer Help Guide

This is a typical stop watch, except is in app
format

To start, press 'Start' or chose 'Start' (ctrl-S) from
the 'File Menu'

To stop, press 'Stop' or chose 'Stop' (ctrl-T) from
the 'File Menu'

To lap, press 'Lap' or chose 'Lap' (ctrl-L) from
the 'File Menu'

To reset, press 'Reset' or chose 'Reset' from
the 'File Menu'

The User can save the timing data (total elapsed time
, current lap time AND all previous Lap Times)
via the 'File Menu':  chose 'Save' (ctrl-V)
